About Us

Who We Are

NATURE1618 is a non profit organization, working in collaboration with local communities around the world with the aim to promote environmental awareness, conserve environmental resources and combat climate change.

Background

Realizing the importance of nature for all living beings, degradation in quality of our lives due to lack of nature, quality of nature around us, Nature1618 was established in 2019 with the motivation to give back to nature and decided to work for this cause by spreading awareness, motivating local communities and work with them to establish naturally rich environment.
