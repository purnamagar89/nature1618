RESPECT TOWARDS NATURE

With skills and interests we can make difference.

Our first and foremost objective is to take proper actions to conserve nature for the enrichment of natural resources. With skills and interests to make difference, volunteering and collaborating with local communities will motivate us to reach the goal of the organization. Exploring the relationship between nature and people living together with proper actions will eliminate obtrusive behavior towards the destruction of nature.

FIRST PROJECT

Aghara Tree Plantation

"Nature1618", started and supported a tree distribution and planting campaign in Ghorahi-06, Dang, Nepal. Phase I of the program was successfully carried out on 2021/06/26 in collaboration with a local club, Jagaruk Ekta Yuva Club.

We would like to express our gratitude to all members and locals who participated in this cause. The following are our plans for the neighborhood.
Phase I: A total of 77 avocado plants were distributed to 58 households. (Completed)
Phase II: 350 different plants (approx.) to be distributed. (Planned)
